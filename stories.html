bike park with dylan and brad

I made the trip back to Kamloops this week to see friends and get some shooting done. We were able to catch a few hours of sun in between all of the rain, and so I met up with Dylan Sherrard and Brad Stuart to do a bit of shooting at the bike park.


"Allan McVicar was the first to invite me to collaborate in his efforts to expose the beauty of our riding and our landscape. Something about being involved in such artistic creations as Allan's photos sparked a creative mission for me that has lasted for years and will not soon burn out."   — Dylan Sherrard (Silvia Films)

pinkbike picture of the month

I received Picture of the Month for April on Pinkbike with a photo I shot of Dylan last April. We shot some of the upper bike park as the sun was setting and moved down after it set. We set up pretty quickly on this jump and shot it a few times before it got too dark. It was the only time I got to shoot Mountain Biking in the past year, and the only time I have shot with Dylan in the last two years probably. This photo got us talking again about shooting plans and I can't wait for the summer to get started!
